Fried Boursin Balls

Description

Fried boursin cheese in a small bite-sized package! These fun appetizers are crunchy on the outside, with a delicious warm herby center.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart canola oil
  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 4.23oz pkgs Boursin Garlic and Fine Herbs Cheese Bites

Procedure

  1. Step 1: Pour oil to a depth of 2 inches in a large Dutch oven. Heat oil over medium heat to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: While oil heats, set out 3 wide shallow bowls. Fill them individually with flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs.
  3. Step 3: Working with a handful of bites at a time, gently dredge cheese in flour, and shake off excess. Dip in eggs; let excess drip off. Dredge in breadcrumbs to coat. Place on a small rimmed baking sheet or large plate. Transfer to freezer for 15 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Fry boursin bites in batches, cooking until golden, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Set on paper towel-lined baking sheet. Repeat with remaining bites. Transfer to a platter and serve immediately.
